# Line Dash

A beautiful, swipeable analog dashboard for Bangle.js 2, designed to look like a high-end sports car instrument cluster. 

Line Dash provides a suite of clean, minimalist gauges that you can effortlessly swipe through to check your daily stats. It is heavily optimized to use zero background battery when sensors are not actively being viewed.

## Features

* **Clock:** A clean, easy-to-read analog clock face.
* **Steps:** Tracks your daily steps using a sweeping dial. 
* **Battery:** Displays your remaining battery life on a satisfying 7-to-5 o'clock dial.
* **Heart Rate:** Features a live, color-coded HR Zone gauge. The HR sensor only activates when you swipe to this screen, ensuring zero background battery drain. 

## Controls

* **Swipe Left / Right:** Cycle through the different dashboard gauges.
* **Tap:** Cycle forward to the next gauge.

## Customization

The app includes a comprehensive settings menu where you can configure:
* Which gauges are visible
* Live Heart Rate refresh intervals
* Your personal Age Decade (to accurately calculate your Max HR and HR Zones)
